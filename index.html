About Us

Health care facilities that are established and operated by Tarlac State University to provide primary and secondary health care for students, faculty, staff, and dependents.

We usually offer a range of services, such as medical and dental consultations, emergency and ambulance services, laboratory and diagnostic tests, pharmacy and dietary services, specialty clinics, public health programs, and referrals to tertiary hospitals.

We aim to promote the health and well-being of the university community by providing quality and comprehensive health care that is accessible, affordable, and responsive to their needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

UHS stands for University Health Services, and it is a department that provides health care and counseling services to students, faculty, and staff of a university. UHS offers a range of services, such as primary care, urgent care, immunizations, laboratory tests, pharmacy, mental health counseling, nutrition counseling, and health educationht.

You can access UHS services by making an appointment online, by phone, or in person. You can also walk in for urgent care or emergency situations. You will need to present your university ID card and your health insurance card when you visit UHS. Depending on your insurance plan, you may have to pay a co-pay or a fee for some services.

If you need health care or counseling when UHS is closed, you have several options. You can call the UHS after-hours nurse advice line to get medical advice or referrals. You can also call the UHS after-hours mental health crisis line to speak with a counselor or get connected to emergency resources.

UHS values your feedback and strives to improve its services based on your suggestions and concerns. You can provide feedback or make a complaint about UHS services by filling out an online form, sending an email, calling the UHS office, or speaking with a UHS staff member in person. UHS will review your feedback or complaint and respond to you as soon as possible.

UHS hours and locations may vary depending on the university. You can check the UHS website or call the UHS office to find out the specific hours and locations of UHS facilities on your campus. Generally, UHS is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., and closed on weekends and holidays.
